RECEIVES CSC PRIME-HRM ACCREDITATION

SCHOOLS DIVISION OFFICE OF MAKATI

chools Division Office of Makati was

accorded the Civil Service Commission

Program to Institutionalize Meritocracy

and Excellence in Human Resource

Management (CSC PRIME-HRM)

Accreditation Award last September 18,

2023, during its flag raising activity at the

SDO-Multipurpose Hall.

The award was a conferment of the

CSC Resolution No. 2300711 promulgated

on August 4, 2023, granting SDO-Makati

several privileges and responsibilities,

including the authority to approve

appointments, subject to post audit.This

is also the result of the onsite

assessment by the Human Resource

Policies and Standards Office, CSC

National Capital Region on May 17, 2022

showing that the agency has met the

PRIME HRM Level 2 Maturity Indicators of

Recruitment, Selection, and Placement

(RSP) and Performance Managements

(PM), and is found to have been

continuously compliant with the civil

service laws, rules and regulations in

terms of implementation of other HR

programs and maintenance of HR

records.

Philip C. Apostol, Director, CSC Field

Office- Makati conferred the award to

SDO-Makati through Ma. Evalou

Concepcion A. Agustin, Schools Division

Superintendent.

SDS Agustin commended the people

who worked together to achieve the

award, including the previous Division

Superintendents, Dr. Maria Magdalena

M. Lim, Dr. Carleen C. Sedilla and Mr.

Rita E. Riddle, Assistant Schools Division

Superintendent Jay F. Macasieb, Dr. Neil

Vincent C. Sandoval, Curriculum

Implementation Division (CID) Chief, Dr.

Rosalie A. Trongco, Schools Governance

and Operations Division (SGOD) Chief,

and the rest of the SDO personnel.

SDO-Makati is the first Division in

the National Capital Region to receive

an accreditation from CSC PRIME-HRM.
